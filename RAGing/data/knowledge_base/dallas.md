# City of Dallas Rental Codes and Tenant Rights (Dallas County)

## 1. Chapter 27: Dallas Minimum Urban Rehabilitation Standards (Air Conditioning)
- **AC Operating Window**: Under Dallas City Code Chapter 27, Section 27-11, landlords who provide air conditioning must ensure it is functional from April 1 to November 1.
- **AC Cooling Standards**: The cooling system must be capable of maintaining a temperature of:
  - 85°F (29.4°C) or cooler in all habitable rooms, or
  - 20°F (11.1°C) cooler than the outdoor temperature, whichever is warmer.
  - If the outdoor temperature is 105°F, the indoor temperature must be maintained at no higher than 85°F.
- **Window Screens**: If windows are opened for ventilation, the landlord must provide tight-fitting window screens (at least 16 meshes to the inch) to prevent insect entry.
- **Fines**: Failure to maintain AC is a Class C misdemeanor, and the landlord can be fined up to $2,000 per day by Dallas Code Compliance.

## 2. Dallas Rental Property Registration & Inspection
- **Registration**: Landlords of all rental properties (single-family, duplex, and multi-family) must register their properties with the City of Dallas Code Compliance department annually.
- **Inspections**: Rental properties are subject to random code compliance inspections. If a unit fails inspection, the landlord has a designated number of days (usually 10-30 days) to make repairs before facing civil penalties.

## 3. Landlord Retaliation Protection (Dallas Amendment)
- Dallas local code strengthens state retaliation rules by making it easier for Code Compliance to issue citations to landlords who evict or raise rent on tenants within 180 days of the tenant filing a complaint with Dallas Code Compliance or Dallas 311.

## 4. Local Legal and Advocacy Resources (Dallas/Dallas County)
- **Legal Aid of Northwest Texas (LANWT)**: Provides free civil legal assistance to low-income residents in Dallas. Website: www.lanwt.org. Phone: (214) 748-1234.
- **Dallas Housing Crisis Center (HCC)**: Offers legal clinics, advocacy, and emergency assistance to renters facing eviction. Phone: (214) 828-4244.
- **Dallas Tenants Association**: Local organizing group providing community advocacy and renter support workshops.
