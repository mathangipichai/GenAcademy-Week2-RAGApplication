# City of Aubrey Rental Codes and Tenant Rights (Denton County)

## 1. Heating and Cooling Standards
- **Mandatory Winter Heating**: City of Aubrey ordinances mandate that all rental dwelling units must be provided with heating facilities capable of maintaining a temperature of at least 68°F (20°C) in all habitable rooms, bathrooms, and toilet rooms.
- **Heating Period**: Landlords responsible for supplying heat must maintain this temperature from December 1 to March 1.
- **Air Conditioning**: There is no state law or local ordinance in Aubrey mandating the installation of air conditioning. However, landlords must diligently repair AC units that were provided as part of the rental agreement.
- **Reporting Violations**: Renter complaints regarding heating or electrical violations can be reported to the Aubrey Code Compliance Department at (940) 440-9343.

## 2. Rental Property Registrations
- **Short-Term Rentals (STRs)**: All STR operators (less than 30 consecutive days) must register their property with the City of Aubrey for Hotel Occupancy Tax (HOT) collection.
- **Long-Term Rentals**: Long-term single-family rental properties do not require city-wide licensing or registration.

## 3. Local Legal and Advocacy Resources (Aubrey/Denton County)
- **Legal Aid of Northwest Texas (LANWT)**: Denton Office provides free legal counseling for renters. Phone: (940) 387-5357.
- **Aubrey Code Compliance**: Reports of code violations or habitability issues. Phone: (940) 440-9343.
- **Denton County Friends of the Family**: Emergency housing and support services. Phone: (940) 387-5131.
